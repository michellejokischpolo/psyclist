tips for the PSYclist | try our routes

hydration

stay hydarted by drinking plenty of fluids before, during &
after riding. recommended beverages: water, coconut water, & if
you are feeling reckless - BEER.

taking care of your muscles

remember to stretch before, during &
after riding to avoid injuries to your muscles.

comfort & clothing

if the weather is rainy or snowy: water proof mittens | gloves | raincoat
can help keep you ultra-warm
warm pants, or leggings can help your legs warm & dry.
or shorts that dont ride up -preferrably made of cotton.
a bright colored top to keep you visible.
